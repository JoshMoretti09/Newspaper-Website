Philadelphia

The City of Brothery Love

A City filled with History

Founded by William Penn in 1682 to serve as capital of the Pennsylvania Colony. During the American Revolution, Philadelphia played an instrumental role as a meeting place for the Founding Fathers of the United States, who signed the Declaration of Independence in 1776 and the Constitution in 1787.

A City filled with Food

Fine dining in philadelphia has never been better with famous restaurants such as Buddakan, The City Tavern and realativiy new Steakhouses like the Barclay Prime home of the Barclay Prime Cheesesteak which is an interesting take on a classic Philly Cheesesteak served with a bottle of Champagne.
